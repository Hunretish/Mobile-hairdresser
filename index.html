What is a coleus?

A coleus is a leafy colorful plant that's easy to grow and maintain in most climates.
It has gained popularity in recent years due to its versitality, its vivid colours and
how easy it is to propagate. Taking seeds from your own or a friends' plant and watching
it grow to look like the motherplant or an entirely new variant has given joy to thousands
of plant enthusiasts over the years!
