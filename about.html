About Us

Mobile hairdressers

Spending hours at the salon is not always ideal. We offer a mobile hairdressing service where we bring the salon to you. We are passionate about making hair appointments fun. We customize all of our services to fit the clients occasion.

We do regular at home services but also cater to social events such as weddings, modelingshows etc.

Our team consists of qualified hairstylists with long experience working with ALL hairtextures. We do haircuts, hairstyling and colouring. Check out our gallery for inspiration of our work.

For booking please contact us and we will set up an appointment for further discussion of your visions.
